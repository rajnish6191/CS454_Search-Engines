        Cats Protection is the UK’s leading feline welfare charity. In 2014 we helped over 205,000 cats and kittens through our national network of over 250 volunteer-run branches and 32 adoption centres.
         

        Gifts in wills

        Find out how remembering Cats Protection in your will can make a huge difference to the lives of the cats and kittens in our care for many years to come.

        Neutering

        Find out about our neutering initiatives and whether you qualify for financial assistance to help neuter your cat. Also discover more about neutering ferals and multi-cat households.
